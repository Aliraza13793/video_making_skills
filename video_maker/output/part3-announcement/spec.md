# Video Spec: AgentFactory Part 3 Announcement

## Intent
- **Purpose**: Announce and introduce AgentFactory Part 3 - Business Domain Agent Workflows
- **Audience (Primary)**: Enterprise decision-makers (CFOs, VPs, operations leaders) — people who control budgets and adoption
- **Audience (Secondary)**: AI practitioners and domain professionals who will implement
- **Key Message**: Part 3 shifts from AI infrastructure to production-ready enterprise deployment — domain expertise is the scarce resource, not technical capability

## Creative Direction
- **Vibe**: Professional, enterprise-ready, announcement-style with momentum — authentic over over-polished
- **Camera Style**: Dynamic transitions, bold typography, terminal-inspired elements, spring physics only
- **Color Palette**: AgentFactory classic tech (navy #1C2833, slate #2E4053, silver #AAB7B8, off-white #F4F6F6)
- **Typography**: JetBrains Mono (display/headlines/cursor), Inter (body/subtext)

## Audio Strategy
- **Music bed**: Subtle bass drone / ambient tech pulse — present but not dominant
- **SFX**: Keyboard typing (hook reveal), transition whooshes (scene changes), subtle click (checklist items), bar chart sweep (governance viz)
- **Voiceover**: Disabled — all messaging conveyed through on-screen text (80%+ of social views are muted)
- **Mix priority**: SFX sit above music bed; nothing competes with text readability

## Scene Breakdown (5 scenes, ~45 seconds total)

### Scene 1: Hook — Brand + Announcement (0-3s)
- **Text (primary)**: `AgentFactory: PART 3_` (typing reveal, blinking cursor)
- **Text (secondary, smaller)**: `NOW LIVE`
- **Visuals**: Terminal-style typing reveal. "AgentFactory" types first, then ": PART 3" types with emphasis. Blinking cursor on "3_". "NOW LIVE" badge fades in top-right corner (green accent or silver).
- **SFX**: Keyboard typing syncs with text reveal, subtle bass hit on completion
- **Transition**: Spring scale-in from center
- **Why**: "PART 3" alone assumes prior context. Adding "AgentFactory" establishes brand for new viewers. "NOW LIVE" creates urgency.

### Scene 2: The Shift — What Changed (3-10s)
- **Text (primary)**: `From AI Infrastructure → Enterprise Deployment`
- **Text (secondary)**: `Parts 1 & 2 taught you to build AI. Part 3 teaches you to deploy it in real business functions.`
- **Visuals**: "AI Infrastructure" appears → arrow animates downward → "Enterprise Deployment" slides in. Subtitle fades below after 1s delay.
- **SFX**: Downward whoosh on arrow, subtle click on subtitle
- **Transition**: Spring fade-in
- **Why**: Establishes the evolution narrative. 7s gives text time to be read (avg 2-3 words/sec for comprehension).

### Scene 3: Core Principle — The Insight (10-17s)
- **Text (primary)**: `"Domain Expertise is the Scarce Resource"_`
- **Text (secondary)**: `Not technical capability. Professional judgment encoded into AI workflows.`
- **Visuals**: Monospace quote with blinking cursor, silver accent on dark navy. Subtitle appears 1s after quote (staggered reveal).
- **SFX**: Single keystroke on quote reveal
- **Transition**: Spring scale-in
- **Why**: This is the sticky line people will remember. Give it room to land.

### Scene 4: Scope + Deliverables — What's Inside (17-33s)
- **Text (primary)**: `16 Chapters | 7 Enterprise Functions`
- **Visuals**: 2x2 or 3x2 grid of function cards (CFO Office, Legal & Compliance, Sales & Marketing, Supply Chain, HR & Operations, Innovation Lab). Cards stagger in (100ms apart). After grid establishes (~2s), cards shrink slightly and deliverables checklist slides in from bottom:
  - ✓ Production-Ready SKILL.md Libraries
  - ✓ Governance Frameworks (10-80-10 Rhythm)
  - ✓ MCP Integration
  - ✓ Multi-Jurisdiction Compliance
- **Text (secondary, beneath checklist)**: `Not prototypes. Deployable agent workflows.`
- **SFX**: Card slide-in clicks, checklist checkmark sounds
- **Transition**: Spring scale-in for grid, spring slide-up for checklist
- **Why**: Merged scope + deliverables + governance into one scene. 16s gives grid time to register and checklist time to read. Explains 10-80-10 inline ("Governance Frameworks (10-80-10 Rhythm)").

### Scene 5: CTA — Action (33-45s)
- **Text (primary)**: `Start Building_`
- **Text (secondary, URL)**: `agentfactory.panaversity.org`
- **Text (tertiary, smaller, description)**: Full link with path in video description / pinned comment
- **Visuals**: Large headline with blinking cursor. Short URL in monospace below. Optional: "Docs → Business Domain Agent Workflows" as tertiary line.
- **SFX**: Final keystroke on "Start Building_", subtle resolve chord
- **Transition**: Spring scale-in
- **Why**: Short URL is readable in 12s. Full path lives where viewers can copy it (description, comments, post caption).

## Distribution Plan
- **Master**: 16:9 (1920×1080) — LinkedIn, YouTube, website embed
- **Cutdown**: 9:16 (1080×1920) — TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts. Reframe: center-aligned text, crop top/bottom of grid scene
- **Thumbnail**: Frame from Scene 1 — "AgentFactory: PART 3_" with cursor visible

## Technical Constraints
- **Target length**: ~45 seconds (7 scenes → 5 scenes, consolidated)
- **Frame rate**: 30 FPS
- **Aspect ratio**: 16:9 master, plan 9:16 cutdown
- **No linear/robotic motion** — spring physics only
- **No emoji icons** — terminal checkmarks (✓) only
- **No slideshow transitions** — spring scale/fade/slide only

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Hook in first **2 seconds** with "AgentFactory: PART 3" + "NOW LIVE" (not 3s — 2026 benchmark)
- [ ] Brand name visible in first frame (new viewers know what this is)
- [ ] Clear value proposition by 10 seconds (the shift: build → deploy)
- [ ] Core principle memorable by 17s ("Domain Expertise is the Scarce Resource")
- [ ] Enterprise scope visible (16 chapters, 7 functions grid)
- [ ] 10-80-10 governance explained inline (not dropped without context)
- [ ] CTA URL readable and copyable (shortened on-screen, full path in description)
- [ ] All messaging works with sound **off** (captions = on-screen text)
- [ ] Audio SFX enhance silent viewing (typing, whooshes add rhythm)
- [ ] No scene under 3 seconds (minimum for text comprehension)
- [ ] spec.md timings match spec.json frame counts exactly
